The primary technical failures are:

The cognitive and pedagogical effort required to move from a 4 to a 5 is exponentially higher thanfrom a 1 to a 2. NPS Misalignment: Net Promoter Score is a proxy for student "likability" and sentiment, not technical competency. Relying on NPS creates an incentive for mentors to prioritize "entertainment" over"rigor," leading to a dilution of the curriculum's depth.
